Biography

I am an AI researcher specializing in generative and representational vision models, especially for medical image analysis. My work spans diffusion models, self-supervised learning, high-resolution imaging, and multimodal alignment.

I have published at MICCAI, MIDL, IEEE ISBI, and IEEE JBHI, with research experience across IIT Madras, GE Healthcare, and NuvoAI.
